About Us

Masjid Bandar Rinching serves as a hub for spiritual growth, community service and Islamic education. Located in the Semenyih, our masjid welcomes people of all backgrounds and provides a speace for prayer, lectures, family events and youth programs.

Our Mission

To nurture a strong, compassionate, and knowledgeable Muslim community through religious guidance, education, and service.

Our Vision

To be a welcoming center of Islamic faith, fostering unity and serving as a beacon of hope and spirituality for all.
